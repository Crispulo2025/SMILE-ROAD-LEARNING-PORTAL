How To Use the Portal

Follow these simple steps to access learning resources.

1

Open the Portal

Visit the Smile Road Learning Portal homepage and browse available learning resources.

2

Select a Playlist

Choose either Videos By Term or Videos By Quarter based on your learning needs.

3

Watch Learning Videos

Open the selected playlist and watch the lessons at your own pace.

4

Review Lessons

Rewatch important lessons and take notes to improve understanding.

5

Continue Learning

Explore more playlists and featured videos to strengthen your skills.

Learning Tips

  • Set a regular study schedule.
  • Take notes while watching videos.
  • Pause and replay lessons when necessary.
  • Practice what you learn.
  • Review lessons weekly.
