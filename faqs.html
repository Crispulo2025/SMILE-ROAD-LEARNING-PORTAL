Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the portal.

What is Smile Road Learning Portal?

Smile Road Learning Portal is an online educational platform that provides learning videos and educational resources.

Do I need an account to view videos?

No. This version of the portal allows users to browse and watch educational content without logging in.

How do I access playlists?

Use the navigation menu and select either "Videos By Term" or "Videos By Quarter."

Can I watch videos on mobile devices?

Yes. The portal is designed to work on smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers.

How can I contact support?

Visit the Contact page and send your inquiry using the contact form or email information provided.

Are the learning materials free?

Yes. Featured videos and learning resources may be accessed freely through the portal.
